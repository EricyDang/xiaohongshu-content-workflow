# QA And Operations

## Publishing QA
- The first page explains the topic or benefit immediately.
- The title matches the actual content.
- The first three pages create either共鸣, clarity, or usefulness.
- The page order has a clear reason.
- Each page can be read on a phone.
- Text is short, concrete, and not generic AI wording.
- The final page gives a natural save, comment, or next-action reason.

## Visual QA
- All images use the same size and visual direction.
- Text contrast is high enough.
- No page is overcrowded.
- No generated typo, random English, logo, or watermark appears.
- No private IP, private artwork, unauthorized character, or trademarked design appears.
- If Chinese text quality matters, use no-text images and add text afterward.

## Privacy And Rights
- Do not expose names, phone numbers, school IDs, resume details, chat records, order IDs, addresses, or faces unless the user has confirmed permission.
- Do not copy private characters or unpublished creative settings.
- Do not imply official endorsement by a brand, school, platform, or employer unless verified.
- For medical, legal, financial, employment, or contract topics, keep advice general and add a professional-consultation boundary when needed.

## Engagement Design
Use one clear interaction goal per post:

```text
comment collection
keyword reply
topic voting
case submission
follow-up request
save checklist
```

Good prompts:

```text
你现在最想解决哪一步？
评论区留关键词，我按呼声整理下一篇。
你更想看新手版还是进阶版？
有没有同样踩过这个坑的人？
```

## Post-launch Review
Track:

```text
曝光
阅读/播放
点赞
收藏
评论
关注转化
封面点击感
前3页留存
评论关键词
```

Review questions:

```text
封面有没有说清楚主题？
标题有没有对应搜索词？
用户收藏的是方法、清单、情绪，还是素材？
评论区有没有暴露下一篇选题？
是否适合做成系列？
```
